— For my father —

Before the boarding pass, before the e-ticket, there was the validation plate — a small embossed card pressed onto carbon tickets at the agency counter. My father kept a drawer full of them well past their useful life. This collection of logos is a small sample of the visuals I got to encounter at the office.

My father, Joseph Winkleman, ran Winkleman Travel in Cranston, Rhode Island, for over thirty years. This site is for the things he had that inspired me, and for the people who remember him.
