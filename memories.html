Tell us a memory

If you knew my father — worked alongside him, booked a trip through him, sat across from him at a long dinner — we'd love to hear a story. Anything. A one-liner he used to say, a trip he planned that went sideways, the way he answered the phone. These go straight to me.

Memory submission form

There is no length limit. Write as little or as much as you want.

Your memory goes privately to the family. Nothing from this form is published on the website. If you'd prefer, you can also email directly — send a note.

Winkleman Travel

My father, Joseph Winkleman, ran Winkleman Travel in Cranston, Rhode Island, for over thirty years. This site is for the things he had that inspired me, and for the people who remember him.
